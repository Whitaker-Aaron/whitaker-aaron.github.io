ABOUT

Nexulum is a 3D top down installment the action/adventure video game genre. Nexulum serves to empower the player by allowing a selection of multiple classes, each that come equipped with their own weapons and abilities. During the player's time in the world of Nexulum, they will be exploring the lost ruins of a fabled age that has forgotten to time in search of a relic dubbed, "The Nexulum". Utilize magical runes and materials found throughout these ruins to further craft and augment your current abilities to become more capable and make it to the peak of the ruins to fufill your mission objective.
